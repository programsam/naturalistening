About Ada

Ada is an Amazingly Accurate and Magical Natural Medium, Emotional Energy Therapist, and Intuitive Teacher. Born with strong intuitive and mediumship abilities in her mid life she felt the urge and need to start learning what her psychic abilities were and how they worked. It’s that inner drive for knowledge that lead her to learn about Psychic abilities, mediumship, energy systems, chakras, energy healing and more. It’s also her innate curiosity that keeps her wanting to learn, share and teach others about this wonderful mystical part of our world we live in.

Ada Loves helping others with her abilities. Over the nearly two decades, Ada has found that the following is very helpful for Mediumship sessions:

  • PLEASE wait at least 3, preferably 6 months before having a mediumship reading with anyone. You have to go through your grief process so that you can process your loved ones answers more clearly and better when you do get a reading.
  • Always have paper and pen to hand during a session as making notes is a great way to focus on the bright points and questions you have from a session.
  • Be in a comfortable and restful place for a Mediumship reading so that you don’t feel rushed, can relax and take in what information is being given.

For Psychic and Mediumship readings making notes is important too, as Ada is more of a prescriptive Psychic. This means she is more apt to help you to avoid or work around difficulties than predicting a “happy ever after” because we all get bumps, bruises and bloopers in life. Some things we can lessen if we have a little help along the way with this type of prescriptive intuition.
