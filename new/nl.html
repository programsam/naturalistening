Natural Listening

To me Natural Listening is just that, listening to the most natural part of ourselves, our intuition. We are all intuitive. As I tell my students, there is no such thing as a human psychic turnip. We all have gut feelings, hints and little things that happen in our lives. These things we often don't pinpoint as being our intuition, but they are.

Here are just a few ways your intuition may be working with you too.

  • Do you have Deja vu experiences at times?
  • Can you see just the right shades of a color to match with something you already have?
  • Do you sometimes hear sounds you cannot explain like whispers just out of range, bells, or other things?
  • Have you seen things out of the corner of your eye you've tried to explain away but can't?
  • Have you ever walked into a room, house or building and felt strong emotional vibes or just felt there was something odd in that space but you couldn't put your finger on it?

These are all signs of your intuition at work for you believe it or not!

One of the things that I most fervently want you to understand is that these and other experiences are all perfectly "normal" and natural. They are a part of you as much as the physical self you were born with! There are ways you can understand these amazing abilities even more, as well as shield from energies you want to keep away from your energy field.
